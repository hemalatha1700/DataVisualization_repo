Countries Table Dataset

country: The name of the country. rank: The rank of the country based on a specific metric (not specified). area: Total area of the country in square kilometers. landAreaKm: Land area of the country in square kilometers. cca2: Two-letter country code. cca3: Three-letter country code. netChange: Annual net change in population (percentage). growthRate: Annual population growth rate. worldPercentage: Percentage of the world population. density: Population density per square kilometer. densityMi: Population density per square mile. place: Numeric code for the country (possibly a standard identifier). pop1980 to pop2050: Population numbers for the years 1980, 2000, 2010, 2022, 2023, 2030, and 2050.

COVID Data Dataset

iso_code: ISO code for the country. continent: Continent on which the country is located. location: Name of the country. date: Date of the data record. total_cases: Cumulative number of COVID-19 cases. new_cases: Number of new COVID-19 cases reported on the date. total_deaths: Cumulative number of COVID-19 deaths. new_deaths: Number of new COVID-19 deaths reported on the date. icu_patients: Number of COVID-19 patients in ICU on the date. hosp_patients: Number of COVID-19 patients hospitalized on the date. (Additional columns related to COVID-19 testing, vaccinations, and other metrics)

World Data 2023 Dataset

Country: The name of the country. Density (P/Km2): Population density per square kilometer. Abbreviation: Abbreviation or short form of the country's name. Agricultural Land (%): Percentage of land used for agriculture. Land Area(Km2): Total land area of the country in square kilometers. Armed Forces size: Size of the country's armed forces. Birth Rate: Birth rate per 1,000 people. Calling Code: International calling code for the country. Capital/Major City: Capital or a major city of the country. Co2-Emissions: Annual CO2 emissions. CPI: Consumer Price Index. CPI Change (%): Yearly change in the Consumer Price Index. Currency-Code: Currency code. Fertility Rate: Average number of children a woman will have. Forested Area (%): Percentage of land covered by forests. Gasoline Price: Price of gasoline per liter. GDP: Gross Domestic Product. Gross primary education enrollment (%): Percentage of eligible children enrolled in primary education. Gross tertiary education enrollment (%): Percentage of eligible individuals enrolled in tertiary education. Infant mortality: Infant mortality rate per 1,000 live births. Largest city: Largest city in the country. Life expectancy: Average life expectancy at birth. Maternal mortality ratio: Maternal deaths per 100,000 live births. Minimum wage: Minimum wage (not specified if hourly, daily, or monthly). Official language: Official language(s) of the country. Out of pocket health expenditure: Out-of-pocket health expenditures. Physicians per thousand: Number of physicians per thousand people. Population: Total population of the country. Population: Labor force participation (%): Percentage of the population participating in the labor force. Tax revenue (%): Percentage of GDP collected as tax revenue. Total tax rate: Total tax rate as a percentage of commercial profits. Unemployment rate: Unemployment rate as a percentage. Urban_population: Urban population number. Latitude: Geographic latitude. Longitude: Geographic longitude.
